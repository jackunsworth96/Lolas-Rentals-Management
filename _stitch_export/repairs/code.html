Common Issues & Self-Fix Tips

Check these quick fixes before calling—they might save you a wait!

tire_repair

Flat Tyre

Slowly pull over. Do not ride on a flat as it ruins the rim. Check for nails or thorns.

Call support for mobile repair
battery_alert

Dead Battery

Check if lights were left on. Try kick-starting (if available) or wait 5 mins and try again.

Check kickstand is UP
local_gas_station

Running out of Fuel

The gauge can be tricky. If it sputters, find the nearest "Coke bottle" fuel stall (Sari-Sari store).

Use Unleaded only
settings_suggest

Chain Came Off

Common on semi-autos. If safe, use a stick to guide the chain back onto the rear sprocket while rotating the wheel.

Watch your fingers!
block

Scooter Won't Start

Ensure the kickstand is fully up and you are squeezing the brake lever while pressing the start button.

Check Kill Switch (Red button)

Island Safety Tips

terrain

Road Conditions

Watch out for sand on corners and potholes, especially after it rains. Take it slow!

cloudy_snowing

Driving in Rain

Roads get very slippery instantly. Reduce speed by half and avoid sudden braking.

pets

Dogs on the Road

Local dogs often sleep on the road. Be alert, they move slowly or not at all.

dark_mode

Night Driving

Street lights are rare outside GL. Drive slower and watch for pedestrians and unlit bikes.

surfing

Surf Rack Safety

Ensure your board is strapped tightly. The extra width can catch the wind on breezy days.
